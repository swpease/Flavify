Philosophy

The point of Flavoration is to expand your cooking horizons. There are no recipes, no reviews, no public comments. Our main goals are:

  • Encouraging adventurousness in the kitchen.
  • Decreasing blind reliance on recipes.
  • Encouraging you to share your favorite flavor combinations with the world.

A problem with recipe websites is that reviews tend to be unhelpful. Do any of these sound familiar:

  • [Five star review] Can't wait to try this!!
  • It's okay... I didn't have any allspice, and I upped the cinnamon by a tsp, and substituted fennel for clove, and dried apricots for chocolate, and...
  • HOW DARE YOU PUT DAIRY IN BOLOGNESE!!!
With that in mind, we wanted to keep things simple: just a table of combinations, and whether people like those combinations.

Use

All you have to do is pick ingredients from the ingredients picker. Some foods go by more than one name, like crawfish and crayfish, which are noted in the picker with the name they appear as in the table. For instance, "filbert (as hazelnut)" will appear as "hazelnut" in the table.

If you sign up, you'll be able to:

  • Like / dislike
  • Favorite
  • Write notes
  • Submit your favorite flavor combinations
